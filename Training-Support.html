Training & Support

from £130 per month

When it comes to managing charity finances, it can often be challenging to find cost-effective, reliable and available expertise to help maintain your books, especially if you’ve a small team with a growing set of accounts! Over the many years that I’ve worked in the charity sector, I’ve found that sometimes we just need an extra set of expert hands to help out. That’s why I’ve developed three cost effective support packages to help you streamline your accounting processes.

All three support packages listed below require you to either be using, or be wiling to migrate to the excellent ExpensePlus fund accounting software. It’s specifically designed for charities and nonprofit organisations and in my opinion, it is the best system you could choose to save time, money, and stress when it comes to charity bookkeeping.

My day rates for the services below are based on income size, to make things as accessible as possible for charities of all shapes and sizes.
