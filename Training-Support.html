Training & Support

from £250

When it comes to charity finances, it can be difficult to find affordable, reliable, and appropriate support to ensure you’re doing things the right way. At times, it can become overwhelming, especially if you’ve a small team with a growing account! I understand your struggle - I have been through it myself. Sometimes you’ll need to ask for an extra pair of experienced hands. That’s where I come in.

I believe that every charity or socially purposed organisation should have access to high-quality bookkeeping software and the expert support they need to develop streamlined financial processes. From experence, the best way to do that is by using ExpensePlus fund accounting software. This cloud based software is specifically designed with people like you in mind. Every charity in the world should be using it. Once you try it, you’ll find it hard to imagine how you operated without it. It will save you time and money while making your accounting processes so much more efficient. But how do you get to that point?

To help you get to that place as quickly and easily as possible, I offer the consulting, training and support packages listed below. Oh, and my day rates are based on income size, to make things as accessable as possible for you.
