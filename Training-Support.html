from £65

ExpensePlus provides excellent free training and support through their website, which is sufficient for many users. However, sometimes additional support from me is helpful if:

  • You would benefit from bespoke training.
  • Managing charity accounts is new to you and you would like additional help getting started.
  • You need help sorting out your finances because they are complicated and messy.
  • You want to migrate to ExpensePlus as quickly and easily as possible without having to figure it all out yourself.
  • Or for any other reason you like!

      Migration Support Package - What’s included?

      ×

      We will start with a free, no-obligation, 30-minute consultation via Zoom. This will enable me to learn more about you, your organisation, and your current accounting setup in greater depth.

      Each organisation's migration to ExpensePlus is unique, so the fees I charge may vary from the basic package price of £750. This is particularly true if your accounts require significantly more work than usual before they can be transferred to ExpensePlus. However, if I think your accounts are simple to migrate or you are a very small organisation, I may be able to offer a price lower than the base price. I will confirm the package price with you at the end of this session.

      I’ll then send you a migration preparation pack to help you get all the required information ready for your switch.

      When you’re ready, I’ll design and build to a new fund accounting architecture inside ExpensePlus for you. I’ll then carefully migrate all your accounts and funds over to the new system.

      With the migration complete, I’ll do a 2 hour online training session with your bookkeepers to get them up and running smoothly. Alternatively, you may decide to outsource your bookkeeping to me to at this stage to free up your team - more details here.

      Finally, I’ll provide 1 month of priority email support for your team if they have any further questions.
