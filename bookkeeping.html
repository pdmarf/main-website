If you already use ExpensePlus fund accounting software and are thinking about outsourcing your bookkeeping, then we'll start with a free, no-obligation consultation via Zoom. If you choose to go ahead, I will then create a customised weekly bookkeeping process for us. This will clearly define how I will collaborate with your team to ensure that the bookkeeping happens seamlessly in the background for you. Each week, I will check the digitally uploaded receipts, set up payments that you have approved, make adjustments as needed, and reconcile the books.

In addition to answering emails and queries, it is typical to schedule a monthly call to review the previous month's figures and address any questions. If you claim Gift Aid on donations, I can prepare your claim for you using ExpensePlus. At the end of the year, I will complete the accruals and depreciation, and help you liaise with your independent examiner to sign off on the accounts.

    Why outsource bookkeeping?

    ×

    By choosing to outsource your bookkeeping to me using ExpensePlus, you can save your church or charity both time and money. ExpensePlus is a cloud-based accounting system, which enables efficient and streamlined remote collaboration between your team and me on a weekly basis. As an ExpensePlus verified bookkeeper, I have the necessary skills and experience to ensure that your organisation's finances are accurately managed and recorded. Your team can then focus on your charity's core mission while leaving the financial day-to-day management to me.
