MORE ABOUT ME

I have worked in the charity sector as a finance officer for almost a decade, gaining extensive experience in fund accounting along the way. I have also served on a leadership team of a church for over 8 years, so I understand the unique challenges of managing medium sized charity accounts, often with only a small or volunteer team. With that experience, I am now committed to helping other charities efficiently manage their finances in a cost effective way. Whether you need outsourced bookkeeping*, want to streamline your accounting systems, or require training for your finance teams, I can help. Let me assist in taking care of your finances efficiently and accurately, so your team can focus on delivering the primary aims of your charity.

EXPENSEPLUS

I’m an ExpensePlus verified bookkeeper and consultant. No matter how big or small your charity may be, ExpensePlus' easy-to-use, cloud-based, fund accounting software will save you time and money. All my services require your organisation to either be using ExpensePlus already, or be willing to migrate to that system (which I can help you with). I am not employed by ExpensePlus, but have extensive user experience with their product. I enjoy a great working relationship with their team and strongly support the excellent work they do in helping churches and charities manage their finances.
