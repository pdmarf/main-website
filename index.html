Take Control of your Charity's Finances

Take the stress out of charity finances by training your bookkeepers and finance leaders.

I’m Pete Marfleet

I’ve worked as a charity finance officer for nearly a decade and served on a charity leadership team for over 8 years. During this time, I’ve gained extensive experience in fund accounting and charity finance regulations while navigating the challenges of managing finances with small or volunteer teams.

As charities grow, their finances naturally become more complex—handling multiple income streams, restricted funds, and increasing compliance demands. This can quickly feel overwhelming.

That’s why I created the School of Charity Finance—to help organisations like yours confidently manage finances, streamline processes, and ensure compliance without needing a massive budget or team.

With practical resources and step-by-step support, I’m here to help you take control of your charity’s finances so you can focus on driving your mission forward.
