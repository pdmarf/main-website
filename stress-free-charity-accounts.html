Stress Free Charity Accounts

Build Confidence in Bookkeeping in 60 Days

Welcome to the School of Charity Finance!

Managing your charity’s finances shouldn’t feel like an uphill battle. This is your chance to fill the knowledge gap, gain clarity on important processes, and build the confidence you need to help your organisation thrive. Ready to see how? Let’s take a look…

What if managing your charity’s finances felt easy?

For too long, charities have had to rely on their amazing team members to “figure it out” when it comes to bookkeeping. And while “doing your best” is admirable, it often leads to unnecessary stress and risky finances.

But what if that didn’t have to be the case?

Imagine having all the knowledge and tools you need to confidently manage your charity’s books, without spending a fortune or sacrificing months of your life. Picture reducing your stress and empowering your charity to focus on its mission at the same time.

It’s possible, and I’m here to show you how!

Here’s the Plan:

I’m inviting charity bookkeepers and finance team members to join me in building confidence in charity bookkeeping — all within 60 days.

Over six practical online modules — released weekly and accessed on demand — you’ll study at your own pace through video lessons and hands-on resources. Together, we’ll build the skills, knowledge, and confidence you need to manage your charity’s finances the way they should be.

If you’ve found yourself learning on the job, this course will give you the structure, clarity, and support you’ve been missing.

    Why outsource bookkeeping?

    ×

    By choosing to outsource your bookkeeping to me using ExpensePlus, you can save your organisation both time and money. ExpensePlus is a cloud-based accounting system, which enables efficient and streamlined remote collaboration between your team and me on a weekly basis. As an ExpensePlus verified bookkeepers, we have the necessary skills and experience to ensure that your organisation's finances are accurately managed and recorded and I'm able to provide ongoing advice to ensure a future-proof accounting system for you. Your team can then focus on your charity's core mission while leaving the financial day-to-day management to us.
